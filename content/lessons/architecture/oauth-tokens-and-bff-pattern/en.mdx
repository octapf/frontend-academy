# OAuth, tokens, and the BFF pattern from the frontend

## What you’ll take away

Access tokens should not live in XSS-readable storage when you can use HttpOnly cookies via a BFF.

## Details

A BFF (Route Handler or internal API) turns session cookies into upstream calls with server-side credentials.

## Recommended practice

- Write down **one real case** from your repo where this topic showed up (or should have).
- When relevant, use **DevTools** (network, console, performance, layout) to connect the idea to what you can observe.

## Common pitfalls

- Shipping `client_secret` or refresh tokens in the bundle via mis-copied config.

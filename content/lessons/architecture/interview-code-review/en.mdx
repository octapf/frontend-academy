# Interview code review (what reviewers look for)

## What you’ll take away

How to **talk through** a diff: intent, trade-offs, risks, and next steps — not just “good” or “bad”.

## Details

Many interviews use an intentionally incomplete snippet: they want **clear questions**, **edge case** awareness (null, empty lists, races), baseline **accessibility** (keyboard, labels), and **observability** (loggable errors). Tie this to team habits: PR size, minimum tests, feature flags.

## Recommended practice

- Ask a teammate for **10 minutes of review** on your PR and note what they asked.
- Pick an older component and list **three improvements** ranked by risk vs effort.

## Common pitfalls

- Huge refactors in the same pass without separating **risk** from **style**.
- Dismissing product context (“this never happens”) without **quantifying** the assumption.

# Selectors and normalized entities

## What you’ll take away

Normalize by id (`entities`, `ids`) for predictable merges and O(1) reads.

## Details

Memoize selectors with `reselect` when deriving filtered lists or totals over large collections.

## Recommended practice

- Write down **one real case** from your repo where this topic showed up (or should have).
- When relevant, use **DevTools** (network, console, performance, layout) to connect the idea to what you can observe.

## Common pitfalls

- Storing the same user in three branches without a single source of truth.

# Slang: team language

This lesson is **read-only**. To practice ES↔EN matching, use the practice block at the bottom.

Tip: pick 3–5 terms you see often and use them in context.

## How to practice

- Say out loud: **ES → EN**, then a **short sentence** using the English term (like a ticket).
- If two translations are valid, pick the one your **company** uses and note the alternative in the margin.

<Callout title="Living slang">
Slang varies by company and region: this list is a **starting point**, not law.
</Callout>

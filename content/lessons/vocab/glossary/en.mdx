# Glossary: key terms

This lesson is **read-only**. To practice, use the practice block at the bottom.

Tip: don’t try to memorize everything. Learn enough to **recognize** the term in PRs, docs, and error messages.

<Callout title="Recognize vs recite">
In interviews and at work, **spotting** the concept and knowing where to go deeper beats textbook-perfect definitions.
</Callout>

## How to study

- **Five terms per day** max; repeat in context (your own sentence, not just the list).
- Cross-check **Reference**: search the term and read the definition out loud.
- Jump to **Slang** when you mix ticket English with team Spanish.

## Long reference lists

In **Reference → Glossary** (`/reference/glossary`) you get the same dataset with **search and pagination** so you can skim or copy definitions without loading the entire list at once.

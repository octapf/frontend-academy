# App Router: segments and dynamic routes

## What you’ll take away

Each folder under `app/` is a segment; `page.tsx` returns UI and coexists with `layout.tsx` in the same tree.

## Details

`[slug]` segments expose `params`; on recent Next versions they may be a `Promise` and need `await`.

## Recommended practice

- Write down **one real case** from your repo where this topic showed up (or should have).
- When relevant, use **DevTools** (network, console, performance, layout) to connect the idea to what you can observe.

## Common pitfalls

- Duplicating routes by mixing `pages/` and `app/` without a clear migration path.

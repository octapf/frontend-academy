# Zod at the boundary: parse the unknown

## What you’ll take away

Define schemas for external payloads and fail fast with clear messages for logs and UI.

## Details

Derive TS types from the schema (`z.infer`) for a single source of truth.

## Recommended practice

- Write down **one real case** from your repo where this topic showed up (or should have).
- When relevant, use **DevTools** (network, console, performance, layout) to connect the idea to what you can observe.

## Common pitfalls

- Parsing on every render without memoizing the schema or caching the result.

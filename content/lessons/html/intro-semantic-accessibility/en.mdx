# Semantic HTML and baseline accessibility

## What you’ll take away

Why **meaningful tags** (`main`, `nav`, `button` vs clickable `div`) improve SEO, keyboard support, and maintenance.

## Details

HTML is the **contract** with the browser and assistive tech: implicit roles, tab order, and alternative text. Good HTML reduces React work: fewer patched `aria-*` later. Interviews often surface the trade-off **“fast with `div`” vs “correct with semantics”**.

## Recommended practice

- Pick a screen from your app and map each block to a **landmark**.
- Try **keyboard only** (Tab / Shift+Tab / Enter) on a form without the mouse.

## Common pitfalls

- `onClick` on a `div` without `role`, focus, or keyboard handling.
- Empty `alt` when the image **does** convey content.

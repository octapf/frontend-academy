# Acceptance criteria and Definition of Done

## What you’ll take away

Good acceptance criteria are observable: “user sees error X when Y fails”, not “make it pretty”.

## Details

DoD includes cross-cutting quality: minimum tests, telemetry, accessibility per team norms.

## Recommended practice

- Write down **one real case** from your repo where this topic showed up (or should have).
- When relevant, use **DevTools** (network, console, performance, layout) to connect the idea to what you can observe.

## Common pitfalls

- Treating a point estimate as a promise without explicit risk callouts.
